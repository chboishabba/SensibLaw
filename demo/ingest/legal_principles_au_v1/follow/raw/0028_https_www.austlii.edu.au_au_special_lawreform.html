About the Library

This library includes databases of law reform reports of all permanent Australian and New Zealand law reform bodies available on AustLII and NZLII. Not all reports are searchable as yet. Catalog and Websearch enables searches of law reform materials on other websites as does Law on Google.

For other law reform databases, see CommonLII - Law Reform and WorldLII - Law Reform.
