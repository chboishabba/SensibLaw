# Wikipedia Revision Contested Pack v1

## Purpose
Add a second curated Wikipedia revision-monitor pack that complements the
ontology/parthood stress pack with live high-contestation pages.

This pack is for:
- volatility-heavy SL/ITIR ingest tests
- revision-delta and epistemic-change pressure testing
- reviewer-support packets on pages where wording and source posture change
  frequently

This pack is not for:
- open-ended crawling
- automatic Wikipedia/Wikidata edits
- replacing the ontology-stress pack

## Why these pages
- They are more likely than the ontology-stress pack to produce meaningful live
  revision churn.
- They pressure different parts of the report surface:
  - narrative drift
  - claim-bearing-step changes
  - attribution/source posture changes
  - issue-packet triage usefulness
- They create a second lane that complements rather than dilutes the ontology
  diagnostic pack.

## Boundaries
- The pack stays curated-only at the manifest level.
- Review context remains explicitly attached from the manifest, with any
  secondary auto-joined bridge context staying clearly labeled and non-authoritative.
- Pages are chosen for revision-monitor utility, not because the pack claims
  they are the globally most contested pages.
